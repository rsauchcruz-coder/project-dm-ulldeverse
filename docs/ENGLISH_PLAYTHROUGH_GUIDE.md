# English companion to the Spanish playable world

The submitted fiction is intentionally set and written in Spanish. All
submission copy, setup instructions and technical evidence are in English. This
companion gives judges an English translation of the interface concepts, the
opening decision and one complete recommended route to a representative ending.

It is a translation aid, not a separate build. The Spanish and English action
labels below identify the same canonical action IDs.

## Opening case

Ulldecona, 1274. The old castle settlement is being dismantled while families
prepare to move into the valley. Guillem Miró, the official responsible for
recording plots of land and water rights, has died after falling into the
cistern.

Mateu Rius admits that he pushed Guillem during a fight, but says Guillem was
still shouting after Mateu was removed. Mateu's ankle chain contains one newly
repaired link, and the transfer order left for the clerk Arnau Cervera is
unsigned. Bernat Llorca is searching the remaining houses for a missing archive
piece and for anyone who spoke with Mateu.

The player, as Arnau, must decide whether Mateu is moved as a prisoner, released
or taken back to the material evidence before Bernat closes the route.

For the clearest demonstration of persistent consequences, choose the third
action. The selection changes visually before confirmation. After execution,
the location becomes the cistern corridor, Miquel appears as a known character,
the recent route updates and the case reveals a fresh seal fragment and scratch
marks beneath the closed cistern lid.

## Meaning of the representative ending

**Dos responsabilidades — Two responsibilities**

The final record keeps three facts separate: altered land-allocation names,
Mateu's admitted push during the fight and evidence that Bernat sealed the
cistern while Guillem could still move. The allocation is suspended, Bernat
loses direct control and Mateu enters an independent custody.

This is not an acquittal. Its value is causal and moral precision: one admitted
act of violence does not erase the later decision that caused Guillem's death.

## What to observe while testing

- The selected action highlights before it is executed.
- Options are derived from the current scene, not from a free-form chatbot.
- Characters, objects and deductions appear only after discovery.
- Resources and earlier decisions change later actions.
- Pressure is expressed through the fiction.
- Restart clears only the local browser play state.
